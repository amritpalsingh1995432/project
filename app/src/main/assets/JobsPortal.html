Jobs Portal

How to Post a Job?

Register as an Employer and then click on the floating button in the right-bottom corner. Then add all the neccessary information and click on "Post Job" button.

How to Apply for a Job?

Register as a Jobseeker and you will see jobs listing on the Home Screen. Click on the job of your interest. A message will appear on the screen "You have applied for particular job".

Updates regarding the Job Fair

Click on the Job Fair Location item fro the menu and you will see the location on the maps with details for the upcoming event.
